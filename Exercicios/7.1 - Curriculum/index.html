Sobre

The main differentiator at any company is it’s employees. Most importantly it takes team players who can thrive, function and excel in a global market. I have these qualities.

My name is Francis Targanski, I am a Account Manager. I have 10 years experience in the commercial sector of multinational companies. I graduated in Architecture and Urban Planning from The Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS) in 2004.

Communication is one of the keys to success and I have the skills to communicate in one of the world's biggest emerging markets: South America. My native language is Portuguese and I have an intermediate level of English.

I am currently a Construction Manager in a project, where I directly manage a team of 15 construction professionals.

Previously I worked as Technical Account Manager at Votorantim Cimentos and ArcelorMittal Brasil S.A., both in the southern region of Brazil (product: Cement and Long Steel), focusing on the development of new B2B markets. In ArcelorMittal i was the Development Project Manager of new products for the railway industry. This project had a focus on the reutilization of steel waste products from automotive industry. The multidisciplinary project had the support of the technical and production areas.

I also worked as Sales Manager and Quality/Environmental Manager for Geosolutions Serviços em Geossintéticos Ltda (Geosolutions Services in Geosynthetics) managing both Commercial and Technical Sections (I was responsible for the sales team in Brazil and South America).

For the past seven years I have had the opportunity to travel extensively in the United States and Europe (Portugal, Spain, Germany, Belgium, Nederland, Luxembourg, Poland, Hungary, Austria, Slovakia, Czech Republic, Finland, Norway, Denmark and Sweden). I therefor have a good understanding of the culture.

If you are ready to work with a talented team player, please contact me.
